Featured in The Indian Express:
Our QuickTake Photobooth was recently covered in a detailed article by The Indian Express, highlighting the revival of vintage photo booths in Bengaluru using the rare Apple QuickTake 100 camera. The article explores the inspiration behind the project, the challenges of sourcing and restoring vintage technology, and how our booth blends nostalgia with modern convenience. It also shares the story of how the booth was designed to recreate the authentic experience of classic photo booths, right down to the look and feel of the 1970s and 80s, and the technical journey of making the Apple QuickTake 100 work in a modern setting.
